ADD Rq Rn Rm - Adds Rn and Rm, places the result in Rq.
ADC Rq Rn Rm - Adds Rn and Rm, places the result in Rq. Also adds carry bit.
SUB Rq Rn Rm - Subtracts Rm from Rn, places the result in Rq
SBC Rq Rn Rm - Subtracts Rm from Rn, subtracts carry bit, puts result in Rq
RSB Rq Rn Rm - Subtracts Rn from Rm, places the result in Rq
RSC Rq Rn Rm - Subtracts Rn from Rm, subtracts carry bit, puts result in Rq
MUL Rq Rn Rm - Rq = Rn * Rm
DIV Rq Rn Rm - Rq = Rn / Rm
RDV Rq Rn Rm - Rq = Rm / Rn
AND Rq Rn Rm - Rq = Rn & Rm
EOR Rq Rn Rm - Rq = Rn ^ Rm
ORR Rq Rn Rm - Rq = Rn | Rm
NOR Rq Rn Rm - Rq = !(Rn ^ Rm)
NAD Rq Rn Rm - Rq = !(Rn & Rd)
NOT Rq    Rm - Rq = !Rm
